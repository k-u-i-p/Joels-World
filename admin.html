Controls & Info

  • Keyboard: Arrow keys to move and turn.
  • Mobile: Use the left virtual joystick to move and the right virtual joystick to turn.
  • Interact: Press Spacebar (or the Talk button on mobile) to talk to friends like hector .
  • Chat: Press Enter to type a message to other players.
  • Fun Commands: Type /dance, /fart, /dead, or /cry in the chat box!
